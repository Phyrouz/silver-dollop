How to Use Cinema Aggregator

Landing Page

Every day you get a new Movie of the Day suggestion matched to your taste, plus a memorable quote from that film. The landing shows a cinema-themed background with the movie suggestion on the left and its quote on the right. Click anywhere or press any key to enter the app.

All Movies

Browse all movies currently showing at BFI Southbank, Prince Charles Cinema, and The Garden Cinema.

  • Search — type to filter by title, director, genre, or venue
  • Venue / Genre filters — narrow down by cinema or genre
  • Min Score slider — only show movies above a taste score threshold
  • Sort buttons — order by taste score, title, year, venue, or next showing date
Click the button above to load a preview

Taste Score & Gauge

Each movie has two scores shown in the circular gauge:

  • Number inside the circle — absolute taste score (1-10) based on how well the film matches your profile: directors you love, genres, themes, era, and origin
  • Percentage next to it — percentile ranking using a gaussian (bell curve) distribution across all movies. Most films cluster around 40-60%, with only the best and worst at the extremes
  • Click the i button next to any score for a full explanation of how scoring works
Click the button above to load a preview

Week Planner

Plan your cinema week:

  • Select a week start date
  • Choose how many movies you want to see (1-7)
  • Tick exclude working hours to skip Mon-Fri 8AM-5PM screenings
  • Click Generate Plan — the planner picks the highest-scored movies with available showtimes that week, avoids time conflicts, and allows up to 2 films on weekends
Click the button above to load a preview

Viewed

Tick the checkbox next to any movie to mark it as viewed. Viewed movies appear greyed out with strikethrough in the main list and are collected in the Viewed tab. Click any card in the Viewed tab to un-mark it.

Your viewed list is saved in your browser and persists between visits.

Click the button above to load a preview

My Schedule

Add movies to your personal schedule, mark them as booked, and track your cinema visits. Export events to Google Calendar with one click.

Click the button above to load a preview

Add Sources

Add new cinema listings:

  • Upload BFI PDF — upload a BFI Southbank programme guide
  • Fetch Prince Charles / Garden Cinema — refresh web listings
  • Custom URL — add any cinema website
  • Upload any PDF — try to extract listings from any programme

Sources are refreshed by running the Python script: python3 cinema_aggregator.py --all --output cinema.html

Click the button above to load a preview

Refreshing Data

You can add movies directly from the Add Sources tab: upload PDFs, fetch cinema websites, type movies manually, or upload CSV files. Everything happens in the browser.

Alternatively, for a full rebuild run: python3 cinema_aggregator.py --all --output cinema.html
